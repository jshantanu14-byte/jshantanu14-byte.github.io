About Me

Finance with a global perspective

I'm a Financial Analyst at illumynt, where I work closely with the CFO on the numbers that shape how the business moves. My work covers revenue and margin reporting, budgeting cycles, cost analysis and scenario modelling, all aimed at giving leadership reliable information when it matters most.

Finance has taken me far. I've studied and worked across India, Thailand, France and the United States, starting with a Siemens internship in Bangkok during my undergrad, building equity models in Jaipur, and financial data work at a fintech in Noida. I went on to lead a research project for UniCredit in New York.

I completed dual Master's degrees from SKEMA Business School, including a final year at North Carolina State University earning a Master's in Finance.

I hold the CFA Charter and passed all three levels, scoring in the top 10% throughout. Three years of focused effort alongside full-time work, and it fundamentally shaped how I think about investment analysis, risk and valuation.
